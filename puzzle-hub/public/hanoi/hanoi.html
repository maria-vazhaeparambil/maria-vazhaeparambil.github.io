Tower of Hanoi

How to Play

Tower of Hanoi is a classic logic puzzle game where your goal is to move all the disks from the first tower to the third tower in the correct order.

  • Click "Play Game" to begin with a set of disks stacked on the first tower.
  • You can only move one disk at a time.
  • Only the top disk of any tower can be moved.
  • You may not place a larger disk on top of a smaller one.
  • Use drag and drop to move disks between towers.

Try to complete the puzzle in the fewest number of moves. Think ahead and plan your moves carefully!
