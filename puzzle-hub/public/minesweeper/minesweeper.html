Minesweeper

How to Play

Minesweeper is a single-player puzzle game where your goal is to uncover all non-mine cells on a grid without clicking on a mine.

  • Choose a board size to start the game. The number of hidden mines will be based on the board size.
  • Left-click a cell to reveal it. If it's a mine, the game ends!
  • Numbers indicate how many mines are adjacent to that cell (including diagonals).
  • If you think a cell contains a mine, right-click to flag it. Flags help you avoid accidental clicks.
  • Clear all safe cells to win the game!

Use logic and deduction to safely clear the board. Good luck!
