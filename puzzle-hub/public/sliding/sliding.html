4x4 Sliding Puzzle

How to Play

The Sliding Puzzle (also known as the 15 Puzzle) is a classic single-player game played on a 4x4 grid with 15 numbered tiles and one empty space.

  • Click a tile adjacent to the empty space to slide it into that space.
  • The goal is to arrange the tiles in order from 1 to 15, with the empty space in the bottom-right corner.
  • Use logic and strategy to minimize your number of moves.
  • Click the "Shuffle" button to start a new random puzzle.

Try to solve the puzzle in as few moves as possible. Good luck!
